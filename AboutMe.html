About me

Hi! I am Zoi, a passionate data professional on an unwavering mission to transform lives and empower policymakers with data-driven insights. Through the power of data, I amplify voices, drive change, and unleash the potential of every story. With expertise in survey design, data analysis, visualization, reporting, monitoring and evaluation, and project management, I extract actionable insights from complex datasets, guiding informed decision-making. Together, let's make a lasting impact.

Here are some highlights of my career path:

  • In 2015, I joined a small local NGO in Athens and embarked on a journey across Greece to collect data and measure the social impact of programs implemented by 9 NGOs in 4 cities. Alongside my colleague, we conducted interviews with 46 NGO stakeholders and surveyed 300 beneficiaries, capturing their stories. Our collaborative efforts resulted in 9 insightful reports that unveiled the transformative power of these initiatives.
  • As the Refugee Crisis significantly impacted Greece, I found myself at the heart of change from 2016 to 2018. Guiding the ECHO Emergency support project for 24 intense months, I established the groundwork for a robust M&E (monitoring and evaluation) system, empowering program teams and leading them towards impactful transformations. By setting indicators and developing log frames, I paved the way for transparent tracking and assessment of program progress and impact.
  • In 2018, my passion for data-driven decision-making led me to join a consultancy firm specializing in official statistics. With a strong aspiration to work with data that policymakers relied on to shape effective interventions, I engaged in numerous data consultancy projects. The highlight of my journey was my role as a data journalist for Eurostat, where I led a small team and authored over 100 engaging data articles. By transforming raw insights into compelling narratives, my aim was to empower policymakers and foster informed decision-making.
  • Motivated by a strong desire to create real impact, in 2022 I embarked on a new adventure and moved to Jordan to work with UNHCR. Overseeing surveys and data research projects, I leveraged my expertise in strategic planning, data analysis, reporting, and stakeholder engagement. Utilizing data analytics and visualization tools, I extracted and manipulated information to provide comprehensive insights that drove informed decision-making and had a positive impact on lives.

Throughout each stage of my career, I have been driven by a thirst for knowledge, the power of data, and a firm belief in the potential of every individual's story to drive change.

Together, let's make a lasting impact!

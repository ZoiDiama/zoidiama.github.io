Survey data:Analysis, visualization & reporting

During my time at UNHCR, I had the incredible opportunity to contribute to a significant survey analysis project that aimed to understand and address pressing issues affecting displaced populations. In this project, I played a pivotal role in analyzing and visualizing the data, which allowed us to uncover valuable insights and present them in a clear and impactful manner. This project involved delving into complex datasets, applying statistical techniques to extract meaningful insights, and transforming the findings into clear and impactful visual representations. I used R to analyze the data and Adobe Illustrator and InDesign to present the results. Below you will find the links to the four reports, I produced with my colleagues for this project.

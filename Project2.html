Data journalism for Eurostat

In my role as a statistical consultant at a consultancy firm, I led a team of two members in a project for Eurostat's "What's New" section, section. From 2020 to 2021, we analyzed data, crafted data articles, and collaborated with talented graphic designers using Adobe Illustrator, while for many artciles I was the main author.Our efforts produced over 100 articles and a multitude of captivating visuals, all proudly published on Eurostat's website.

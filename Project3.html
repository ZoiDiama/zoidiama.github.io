#TidyTuesday: Data visualization with R

Tidy Tuesday is a popular data science project that originated within the R and data science community. It is an online initiative that encourages individuals to practice their data wrangling and visualization skills using real-world datasets. Every Tuesday, a new dataset is shared on the Tidy Tuesday website or GitHub repository. The datasets cover a wide range of topics, including social issues, sports, entertainment, and more. Participants are invited to explore the dataset, clean and organize the data using tidy principles, and create visualizations or analyses to gain insights and tell a compelling story.

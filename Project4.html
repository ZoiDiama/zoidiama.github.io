Tableau public portfolio

The majority of my Tableau Public visuals are dedicated to the #B2VB challenge. Back to Viz Basics (B2VB) is a Tableau Community Project, initiated and guided by members of the DataFam community. Participants have the opportunity to learn, practice, and apply their data skills across a diverse range of topics, products, and causes. Back to Viz Basics emphasizes mastering one chart or visualization principle at a time, providing a focused and immersive learning experience.

Official statistics with R
Reproducing Eurostat's graphs

Inspired by my experience with Eurostat (see this post, for more details), I decided to further enhance my skills by recreating some of the visuals using R for my personal archive. My goal was to reacreate the exact same visual or in same cases I created new ones. Exploring various visualization techniques, I gained valuable insights into effectively utilizing R to create a diverse range of data visualizations. All the data used in the project are freely accessible to the public.
